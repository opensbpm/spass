Data Property: hasPriorityNumber

Annotations (1)

  • rdfs:comment "Transitions or Behaviors have numbers that denote their execution priority in situations where two or more options could be executed.

    This is important for automated execution.

    E.g. when two messages are in the in-box and could be followed, the message denoted on the transition with the higher priority (lower priority number) is taken out and processed.

    Similarly, SubjectBehaviors with higher priority (lower priority number) are to be executed before Behaviors with lower priority."     @en
